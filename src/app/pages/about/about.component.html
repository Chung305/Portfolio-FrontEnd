Background

Chung is originally from Miami where he graduated from Miami Beach Sr. High School. There he acquired a passion for DJing alongside two close family members. Chung’s interest in DJing primarily resided in the hardware used to mix the music, such as CDJs 2000 and Allen Heath Xone: 92.

He eventually moved to Delaware with his brother. There he discovered his desire to pursue a career in technology and chose to begin an education in Computer Science. In studying Computer Science at the Delaware Technical Community College he realized, that like DJing, software development is a passion of his. Chung found himself eagerly and enthusiastically wanting to learn different technologies.

Chung’s work ethic can best be described as creative, detail-oriented, and dedicated. Attempting to change careers and enter the technology field he sought alternative academic pipelines. In refusing to give up, Chung applied for, enrolled in, and completed a 12-week immersive software development program called Zip Code Wilmington. At Zip Code Wilmington, Chung’s work ethic was tested like never before dedicating an entire 3 months, approximately 100 hours a week, to learning Java, object-oriented programming, agile/scrum methodology, TDD, designing interfaces, and working in teams.
